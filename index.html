Social Impact Model Canvas

Global Digital MUN

Democratizing Access to International Diplomacy Education

Problem

  • • Traditional MUNs are expensive ($100-500 per conference)
  • • Geographic barriers limit participation
  • • Limited frequency (1-2 times per year)
  • • Lack of consistent skill development
  • • Many talented students can't afford or access quality MUN experiences

Solution

  • • Fully digital MUN platform
  • • Tiered subscription model ($29-79/month)
  • • Twice-monthly conferences
  • • Global accessibility (any country, any timezone)
  • • Expert moderation & mentorship
  • • Premium resources & networking

Impact

  • • 500+ students engaged annually
  • • 20+ countries represented
  • • 80% cost reduction vs traditional MUNs
  • • Enhanced public speaking & diplomacy skills
  • • Increased global citizenship awareness
  • • Career pathways in international relations

Customer Segments

  • • High school & university students interested in MUNs
  • • Schools & clubs seeking virtual diplomacy programs
  • • Parents & educators investing in skill development

Revenue Streams

  • • Subscription plans: $29/month (basic), $49/month (standard), $79/month (premium)
  • • Sponsorships & partnerships
  • • Certificate & premium workshop fees

VALUE PROPOSITION

"We make Model United Nations accessible, affordable, and impactful for students worldwide"

Through our digital platform, students gain consistent access to high-quality diplomatic simulations, develop critical 21st-century skills, and join a global community of future leaders—all at a fraction of the cost of traditional MUNs.
